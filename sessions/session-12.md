## Session Summary
The Boy and Cal Tir return to Phandalin after the disastrous events at Conyberry, discovering that weeks have seemingly passed despite their experience feeling like only days. They report the deaths of Rurik, Baras, and Valen to Sildar Hallwinter and Sister Garaele, prompting grief and concern.

Phandalin has changed — now guarded and under pressure from repeated Goblin attacks. Reinforcements from the Lord’s Alliance have arrived, including Hashon Saddar and Kuraken Goldkettle, who quickly become entangled with the party in a tense and chaotic tavern meeting.

After drinks, arguments, and an arm wrestling match, the group forms a new five-person party. They decide against immediately returning to Conyberry, instead heading to Thundertree in search of guidance. Along the way, they rescue a drowning Deep Gnome and encounter the dangers of the ruined town — culminating in a fight against undead.
