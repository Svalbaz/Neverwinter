## Session Summary
The party set out along the Triboar Trail towards Conyberry, encountering minor survival challenges and a travelling caravan along the way. After an awkward but ultimately friendly interaction with the caravan, the group defended them from attacking plant creatures.

Continuing onward, the party reached the ruined town of Conyberry, where each member experienced disturbing, personal visions tied to their past. As the visions faded, a mysterious shadowy figure appeared, drawing them deeper into the haunted town where spirits began to manifest.
