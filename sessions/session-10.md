## Session Summary
As the party arrived in Conyberry, they experienced disturbing visions representing their deepest fears before encountering hostile undead.

They fought Shadows, a Spectre, and a Ghast, discovering that some enemies could not be harmed by conventional attacks. The spirits appeared confused and distressed, hinting at a past tragedy.

Further exploration revealed strange illusions, unstable structures, and lingering magical effects throughout the town. As they approached a stone bridge, their visions intensified, and a large shadowy figure briefly appeared.

Crossing into the town triggered further encounters with spectral entities. After defeating them, the party discovered a shrine-like statue surrounded by valuable offerings radiating overwhelming magical energy.

When The Boy attempted to take an item, a large number of spectral entities—including a Banshee and will-o’-wisps—emerged. Returning the item briefly calmed the situation, but a miscast spell re-aggravated the spirits, leaving the party surrounded as the session ended.
