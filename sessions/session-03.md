# Greyhawk – Session 03

**Date:** Wednesday 28th January 2026  
**Location:** Phandalin & Surrounding Area  
**Party Level:** 1

---

## Party Present
- Cal Tir — Elf Sorcerer
- Confessor Baras — Human Cleric
- Rurik Oathless — Dwarf Fighter
- Unknown — Tiefling Barbarian
- Valen Wildheart — Elf Fighter

---

## Quest / Objective
- Escort a caravan to **Phandalin**
- Discover the fate of **Gundrin Rockseeker**
- Investigate goblin activity and growing unrest in the region

---

## Session Summary
The party secured the survival of Sildar Hallwinter and learned of wider political forces at play through the Lord’s Alliance. After safely delivering the caravan to Phandalin, they uncovered a town plagued by lawlessness, intimidation, and disappearances. Multiple leads presented themselves, culminating in the discovery of a hidden tunnel believed to be connected to the Redbrands.

---

## Encounters & Events

### Sildar Hallwinter
- Sildar Hallwinter identified himself as a member of the **Lord’s Alliance**
- *The Boy* attempted to rouse Sildar by shaking him — unsuccessfully
- A healing potion was poured down Sildar’s throat, successfully reviving him
- Sildar revealed:
  - **Iano Albrech**, a wizard of the Lord’s Alliance, was sent ahead to Phandalin
  - Goblins looted the caravan saddlebags
  - **Klarg required a Dwarven map**
  - Gundrin discovered the entrance to an **old cave**
- Sildar believes the map may have been taken to **Cragmaw Castle**
- Sildar suggests:
  - Retrieve the caravan
  - Proceed to Phandalin
- When asked about Cragmaw Castle’s location, Sildar believes **Iano** may know

---

### Journey to Phandalin
- Caravan recovered
- Travel to Phandalin completed without incident
- During the journey:
  - Rurik questioned Baras about his deity
  - Baras revealed he worships **Tyr**
  - Rurik scoffed; conversation ended

## Phandalin

### Arrival
- A villager recommended the **Stonehill Inn**
- The party delivered the caravan to **Barthan**
  - Barthan was distraught upon learning of Gundrin’s fate
  - Revealed that **both of Gundrin’s brothers are also missing**
- Each party member received **10 GP**

- Barthan warned of a local gang:
  - The **Redbrands**
  - Stealing from townsfolk
  - Frequenting the **Sleeping Giant Tap House**

---

### Shrine of Tymora
- Before resting, Baras inspected a shrine
- A poorly constructed shrine to **Tymora**, goddess of luck and good fortune
- An elderly woman was praying there
- Baras donated **2 GP**
- The woman revealed the shrine is maintained by **Sister Gareale**, an elven woman

---

## Stonehill Inn

### Evening at the Inn
- Met **Toblen**, the barkeep
- Rurik purchased a round of drinks and secured rooms
- Sildar retired early
- Rurik dropped his drink when a young barmaid handed it to him

- **Elsa**, the barmaid:
  - Mentioned **Daran Edermath**, orchard keeper and former adventurer

- Toblen’s wife revealed to Baras:
  - **Thel Dendrar**, a local woodcarver, stood up to the Redbrands
  - He was murdered
  - His wife and children were taken
  - **Sister Gareale** lives near the river

- Town leadership:
  - **Harbin Wester**, the Townmaster, provides little security
  - **Halia Thornton** founded the Miner’s Exchange
  - There is no guard or law enforcement presence

- **Pip**, Toblen’s young son:
  - Asked his father for more potatoes
  - Baras gave Pip **1 GP**
  - Pip offered to share a secret
  - Baras warned him to be truthful or face the “bad eye”
  - Baras lifted his eyepatch, frightening Pip
  - Toblen’s wife admonished Baras

- Valen learned from Toblen:
  - The mines are extremely busy
  - A miner named **Lanar** frequents the tavern

- The party retired for the night, feeling refreshed

## The Following Day

- Cal Tir and Valen each purchased a **Healer’s Kit**

### Townmaster & Redbrands
- Baras spoke with **Harbin Wester**, Townmaster, outside the town hall
- Observed a notice:
  - *“Orcs near Wyvern Tor – enquire within”*
- Baras questioned Harbin about the Redbrands
  - Intimidated him into speaking
  - Harbin admitted (to Baras):
    - He has romantic feelings for **Elsa**, the barmaid
    - The Redbrands are a “mercenary guild”
    - He considers them “not much trouble”

- Redbrands:
  - Wear red cloaks
  - Brand their arms

---

### Alderleaf Farm & The Tunnel
- A halfling named **Carp** approached the party
  - Claimed he found a hole in the woods
  - Spotted large, ugly bandits entering it

- **Qelline Alderleaf**, local farmer:
  - Escorted the party through her farm
- Directions given by Carp:
  - Southeast edge of the farm
  - Follow the eastern path
  - Reach the creek
  - Cross north

- The party discovered:
  - A tree where passage had been forced open
  - Rurik led the way
  - Crawled roughly 100 metres through a dark, cramped tunnel
  - Emerged into a cavern
    - Containing a **chasm**
    - A slightly warm wind blowing upward from below
