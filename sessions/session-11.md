## Session Summary

The party continued their battle against overwhelming undead forces, including spectres, shadows, will-o’-wisps, and the banshee Agatha.

The encounter quickly turned into a desperate fight for survival, ultimately resulting in the deaths of Baras, Valen, and Rurik. Only Cal Tir and The Boy managed to escape Conyberry.

### Combat Highlights (Condensed)

* The fight resumes mid-chaos, with the party surrounded by undead.

* Cal deflects a ghast’s attack using his glass staff.

* The Boy and Talon strike back, holding the front line.

* Baras uses divine magic to damage the shadows.

* Agatha rises above the battlefield, her presence overwhelming:

  * Her gaze instils fear — only Cal succumbs.
  * She strikes Valen with **Corrupting Touch**.

---

### The Turning Point

* Rurik, seeing The Boy near death, **throws him to safety toward the river**, ordering him to run and remember Gundren.

* The Boy escapes across the river under heavy pressure.

* Baras is overwhelmed:

  * Before dying, he shouts at The Boy to live — bitter and blaming.
  * In a final act, he **immolates himself**, damaging a spectre.

* Cal casts **Fireball**, thinning enemy numbers but not enough.

---

### Collapse of the Line

* Agatha unleashes a **wail**, instantly killing Cal — though he later miraculously recovers via a critical death save.

* Valen is drained and eventually falls.

* Rurik becomes the final defender, surrounded on all sides.

* Rurik refuses to retreat:

  * Declares someone must “hold the line”
  * Continues fighting despite overwhelming odds

---

### The Last Stand

* Rurik withstands repeated attacks from shadows, spectres, and wisps.

* The Boy desperately calls for him to flee — he refuses.

* Even as enemies bypass him, he continues to fight and protect fallen allies.

* In a final moment, Rurik attempts to save Valen, dropping to his knees beside him.

* A powerful shadow finally kills Rurik, draining his strength.

  * His last word: **“…Cassandra.”**

---

### Escape

* Cal regains consciousness (critical success) and **teleports across the river**, fleeing toward the edge of Conyberry.

* The Boy also retreats, pursued by enemies.

* Both encounter a **magical barrier** surrounding the town:

  * The air appears warped and unreal
  * Cal identifies and partially dispels it

* Cal forces a breach using magic:

  * The Boy struggles through as it closes
  * Both manage to escape

* From safety, The Boy looks back at the battlefield and quietly says:

  > “...I’m sorry.”
