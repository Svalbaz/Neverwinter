## Session Summary
*(High-level recap of what happened this session)*

The party explored the depths beneath Tresendar Manor, triggering traps before cautiously advancing through a corridor marked by a mourning angel carving. They encountered Iarno “Glassstaff” Albrek at a cistern, which quickly escalated into a brutal fight.

Despite heavy damage and multiple party members falling unconscious, Iarno ultimately escaped using magic after delivering a cryptic warning. The party recovered his magical staff and survived the aftermath.

After resting, they explored the ruined manor above, confirming the Redbrands had not been living there directly. Returning to Phandalin, they reported events to Sildar, learned of the Townmaster’s death, and picked up new leads involving Thundertree and a druid named Reidoth.

They also received rewards and information from Halia Thornton and Linene Graywind regarding stolen goods and local factions.
