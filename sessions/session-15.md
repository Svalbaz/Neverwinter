## Session Summary

The party spent the night resting in the abandoned barracks. During the watches, both Hashon and Cal Tir observed mysterious humanoid figures digging among the village mounds in the darkness. Cal was later disturbed by a distant scream that reminded him of his death at the hands of the Banshee.

In the morning, Solas the raven arrived at the barracks and was welcomed inside by The Boy. After discussing the strange events of the night, the party investigated several mounds around the village. Cal detected a magical aura beneath the ground, but despite this discovery the group decided to head north toward the tower believed to be occupied by the dragon.

At the tower, the party discovered two crushed giant spider corpses outside. Inside, thick spider webs clogged the entrance halls. After clearing a path, they found a spiral staircase and encountered what appeared to be Reidoth.

However, Kuraken and Cal quickly noticed subtle inconsistencies in Reidoth's appearance and behaviour. When challenged, the druid's form split apart, revealing Venomfang, a Young Green Dragon, who had been impersonating Reidoth.

Rather than attacking immediately, Venomfang invoked ancient draconic guest-right and offered parlay. During the tense conversation he revealed information about other adventurers, mysterious enemies drawing closer, his former lair, and several cryptic references connected to earlier clues the party had encountered.

Despite several nervous moments and Kuraken's attempt to signal a tactical retreat, the party successfully de-escalated the situation and reached a tentative agreement to assist Venomfang.

### Encounter 3 – Investigating the Mounds

- The Boy had greater success searching the area.

### Encounter 4 – Venomfang's Tower
- The party found two crushed Giant Spider corpses outside the tower.
- Thick spider webs blocked much of the entrance.
- The group encountered what appeared to be Reidoth.
- Kuraken cast Hold Person after noticing inconsistencies.
- "Reidoth" revealed himself to be Venomfang, a Young Green Dragon.

### Encounter 5 – Parlay with the Green Dragon
- Venomfang invoked ancient draconic guest-right.
- He claimed Reidoth had previously sent adventurers to him.
- He referred to "The Enemy Spies" drawing nearer.
- He mentioned his former lair being taken by "The One Who Spins Webs Between Worlds."
- He identified himself as "The Green Lord."
- He referenced descendants of "The One Eye."
- The party avoided combat and agreed to hear his proposal.
