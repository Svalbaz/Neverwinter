## Session Summary

After leaving Venomfang's tower, the party discussed the dragon's suspicious nature and agreed that anything with both "Venom" and "Fang" in its name was unlikely to be trustworthy. They returned to Reidoth seeking answers about Venomfang's references to "The One Eye" and "The One Who Spins Webs Between Worlds."

Reidoth confirmed that green dragons are deceptive and selfish creatures and admitted that the party were the first adventurers he had ever directed toward Venomfang. Although he could provide little additional information regarding the magical corruption beneath Thundertree, he encouraged further investigation.

Using Detect Magic, Cal Tir traced a powerful magical presence south through the ruined settlement. Along the way the party explored an abandoned alchemist's shop, where Kuraken quietly eliminated a Twig Blight and searched for valuables. The Boy remembered Nirna Thendrar's offer regarding her family's heirloom and recovered an emerald pendant necklace from the building.

As the party approached the source of the magic, Cal became increasingly overwhelmed by the magical energies saturating the area. Meanwhile, The Boy and Hashon explored a nearby smithy where they encountered a zombie and a zombie torso.

Following a chaotic battle involving several missed attacks, critical failures, and an improvised capture attempt, Hashon and The Boy transformed the surviving zombie into what they described as a "rudimentary magic detector" by removing its eyes, teeth, and forearms before attempting to use its reactions to locate the magical source.

Using both Cal's magical senses and the zombie's behaviour, the party identified a location to excavate. Digging beneath the ash-covered ruins, they uncovered a large black stone threaded with pulsating red veins. Strange root-like structures extended from the stone throughout the surrounding earth, incorporating materials from the ruined town itself.

Cal connected the discovery to Reidoth's earlier warnings about a corruption beneath the forest. Believing the stone to be the source, he began attempting to dispel or cleanse its magical energy. As arcane power surged through both Cal and the stone, movement echoed throughout the ruins and multiple zombies emerged from the surrounding darkness.
