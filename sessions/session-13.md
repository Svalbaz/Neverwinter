## Session Summary
The session resumed immediately after the party’s battle with an Ash Zombie inside an abandoned tavern in Thundertree. After dispatching the undead creature, the group investigated the tavern and discovered signs that the zombies may have lain dormant for years before being disturbed.

Kuraken attempted — with varying levels of subtlety — to loot valuable alcohol from behind the bar, eventually convincing Cal to use Mage Hand to retrieve several bottles from a high cabinet. Kuraken successfully pocketed both a “cooking wine” and a genuinely fine vintage while trying to disguise the latter from the rest of the party.

The group cautiously ventured deeper into the ruined streets of Thundertree, noting the once-prosperous nature of the town before encountering a pack of animated twig creatures identified as Thicket Stalkers. After a brief but chaotic battle involving Faerie Fire, exploding plant creatures, and a surprisingly effective fake fire illusion from Kuraken, the party emerged victorious.

While exploring further, the group discovered a fortified building that appeared far better maintained than the surrounding ruins. Inside they met Reidoth, an eccentric druid accompanied by a raven named Solas. Reidoth explained that the corruption spreading through Thundertree and the ash-infused undead are tied to the lingering magical imbalance surrounding Wave Echo Cave and the eruption of Mount Hotenow centuries ago.

Although Reidoth admitted he knew the locations of both Cragmaw Castle and Wave Echo Cave, he refused to reveal the latter until the party dealt with a greater threat to the balance of the region: a young green dragon named Venomfang residing in the old tower north of town.

The session ended with the party taking a short rest in Reidoth’s cottage while Kuraken prepared a meal and shared some decidedly less impressive wine.
